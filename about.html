Our Story

From Instagram
to Your Jewellery Box.

Cutewears began as a passion project on Instagram, created with a love for timeless jewellery and meaningful style. What started as sharing beautiful pieces online has grown into a brand trusted by customers who believe that elegance should be a part of everyday life.

At Cutewears, we carefully curate jewellery that is stylish, versatile, and designed to complement every occasion—whether it’s a casual day out, a festive celebration, or a special milestone. We believe that the right piece of jewellery doesn’t just complete an outfit; it tells your story.

Thank you for being a part of our journey and supporting a brand built with passion, creativity, and the love of beautiful details.

Meet The Founder

Diya Sheth

At just 21 years old, Diya Sheth transformed her passion for jewellery and fashion into Cutewears—an Instagram-born brand built with creativity, dedication, and a vision to make elegant jewellery accessible for every occasion.

With a keen eye for trends and a love for timeless designs, Diya personally curates collections that help individuals express their unique style with confidence. Her goal is simple: to create jewellery that makes people feel beautiful, empowered, and celebrated in their everyday moments.

What started as a small dream on social media continues to grow through the trust and support of the Cutewears community.
